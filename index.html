Rainbow Six Siege

Rainbow Six Siege is an tactical first person focused on battles between two teams - one is the attacker and the other defender both sides have 5 people.

In the game, you can play as members of five anti-terrorist units: GIGN (France), GSG9 (Germany), SAS (Great Britain), Spetsnaz (Russia), and SWAT (the United States) in subsequent updates there are more and more "operators" and for example they also added units in subsequent updates: JTF2 (Canada), Navy Seals (United States), BOPE (Brazil), SAT (Japan), GEO (Spain), SDU (China), 707th (South Korea) and GROM (Poland)


The game is for PC, Xbox one and x-series, Playstation 4 and 5.
